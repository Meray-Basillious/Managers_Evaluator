# Complete Assessment Questions & Dimensions - مقيم الأداء

## DIMENSION 1: JOB IDENTITY

### Q1: Current Job Title
**Question (AR)**: ما هو منصبك الحالي؟
**Question (EN)**: What is your current job title?
**Question Type**: Text Input
**Evidence Type**: Optional
**Rating Perspectives**: Self, Manager, Senior Mgmt

---

### Q2: Job Title vs. Actual Responsibilities
**Question (AR)**: لماذا لا ينعكس منصبك الحالي بدقة مسؤولياتك الفعلية؟
**Question (EN)**: Why does your current title not accurately reflect your actual responsibilities?
**Question Type**: Long Text
**Evidence Type**: Required
**Required Evidence**:
- Organization chart
- Job description
- Decisions documentation
- Project leadership evidence

---

## DIMENSION 2: ACTUAL RESPONSIBILITIES

### Q3: List Your Most Important Responsibilities
**Question (AR)**: اذكر أهم 5 مسؤولياتك الأساسية
**Question (EN)**: List your top 5 most important responsibilities
**Question Type**: Multi-item Text Input (5 items)
**Evidence Type**: Optional
**AI Classification**: Each responsibility must be classified as:
- Operational (تشغيلية)
- Supervisory (إشرافية)
- Managerial (إدارية)
- Leadership (قيادية)
- Strategic (استراتيجية)

---

### Q4: Delegation Capability
**Question (AR)**: كم نسبة مسؤولياتك التي يمكن لشخص آخر أن يقوم بها دون الرجوع إليك؟
**Question (EN)**: What percentage of your responsibilities can someone else perform without referring to you?
**Question Type**: Percentage Slider (0-100)
**Evidence Type**: Optional
**Reasoning Required**: Explain why the remaining responsibilities cannot be delegated

---

## DIMENSION 3: TIME ANALYSIS

### Q5: Time Allocation
**Question (AR)**: كيف تقضي وقتك العملي؟
**Question (EN)**: How do you spend your working time?
**Question Type**: Distribution Slider (must total 100%)
**Options**:
- Operational Work: %
- Supervision: %
- Problem Solving: %
- Planning & Strategy: %
- Development & Learning: %
- Training Others: %

**AI Flags**:
- If Operational >70%: "Manager acting as operator" flag
- If Development + Planning >30%: "Leadership maturity" indicator
- If Training >15%: "Talent investment" indicator

---

### Q6: Operational Work Consumption
**Question (AR)**: لماذا يستهلك العمل التشغيلي هذه النسبة من وقتك؟
**Question (EN)**: Why does operational work consume this percentage of your time?
**Question Type**: Multiple Selection + Text
**Options**:
- Lack of trained staff
- Weak delegation practices
- Frequent system issues
- Crisis management (frequent fires to fight)
- Lack of automation
- Personal preference
- Other: ________

---

## DIMENSION 4: AUTHORITY & DECISION-MAKING

### Q7: Independent Decision Authority
**Question (AR)**: ما القرارات التي تتخذها بشكل مستقل؟
**Question (EN)**: What decisions do you make independently?
**Question Type**: Checklist
**Evaluation Criteria**:
- Decision Impact (Strategic, Operational, Tactical, Routine)
- Financial Impact (Amount involved)
- Risk Level (Severity of consequences)
- Frequency (How often)
- Autonomy (Independent vs. consultative vs. delegated)

---

### Q8: Largest Decision in Last 12 Months
**Question (AR)**: أكبر قرار اتخذته خلال آخر 12 شهر؟
**Question (EN)**: What was the largest decision you made in the last 12 months?
**Question Type**: Long Text + Evidence Upload
**Required Evidence**:
- Decision documentation
- Business case (if exists)
- Outcome metrics
- Impact assessment
- Timeline

---

## DIMENSION 5: RESOURCES

### Q9: Number of Direct Reports
**Question (AR)**: كم عدد موظفي الفريق المباشرين؟
**Question (EN)**: How many direct employees report to you?
**Question Type**: Numeric Input
**Sub-questions**:
- Full-time employees: _
- Part-time/Contract employees: _
- Temporary assignments: _
- Total: _

---

### Q10: Budget or Financial Responsibility
**Question (AR)**: ما حجم الميزانية أو المسؤولية المالية التي تدير؟
**Question (EN)**: What is the size of the budget or financial responsibility you manage?
**Question Type**: Numeric Input with Currency Selection
**Include**: Annual amount in currency

---

### Q11: Resource Stability
**Question (AR)**: هل مواردك (فريق وميزانية) مستقرة أم تتغير باستمرار؟
**Question (EN)**: Are your resources (team and budget) stable or continuously changing?
**Question Type**: Scale 1-10 + Text Explanation
**Scale**: 1 (Always Changing) to 10 (Completely Stable)
**AI Analysis**: Calculates Complexity Index based on volatility

---

## DIMENSION 6: ACHIEVEMENTS & RESULTS

### Q12: Most Significant Achievements
**Question (AR)**: ما أهم إنجازاتك وليماذا؟
**Question (EN)**: What are your most significant achievements and why?
**Question Type**: Multi-item (Up to 5 achievements)
**For Each Achievement**:
- What was achieved
- Before state (metrics)
- After state (metrics)
- Business impact
- Sustainability (did results continue?)

**Evidence Type**: Required
- Metrics/Data
- Documentation
- KPI evidence
- Stakeholder feedback

---

### Q13: Results Sustainability
**Question (AR)**: هل النتائج التي حققتها استمرت أم تراجعت؟
**Question (EN)**: Did the results of your achievements continue or did they decline?
**Question Type**: Long Text for Each Achievement
**AI Assessment**: Measures sustainability and real impact

---

## DIMENSION 7: OBSTACLES & SOLUTIONS

### Q14: Main Obstacles
**Question (AR)**: ما أكبر العوائق التي تواجهها في عملك؟
**Question (EN)**: What are the main obstacles you face in your role?
**Question Type**: Multi-item (Up to 5 obstacles)
**For Each Obstacle**:
- Description
- Frequency
- Impact
- Root cause

---

### Q15: Problem-Solving Approach
**Question (AR)**: كيف تتعامل مع العوائق؟ هل تحلها أم تستخدمها كأعذار؟
**Question (EN)**: How do you handle obstacles? Do you solve them or use them as excuses?
**Question Type**: Long Text
**AI Detection**: Identifies problem-solving patterns vs. blame mentality
**Scoring**: Leadership maturity assessment

---

### Q16: Actions Before Escalation
**Question (AR)**: ما الإجراءات التي تتخذها قبل تصعيد المشكلة؟
**Question (EN)**: What actions do you take before escalating a problem?
**Question Type**: Long Text
**AI Assessment**: Measures initiative and problem-solving capability

---

## DIMENSION 8: FUTURE VISION & STRATEGY

### Q17: Department Improvement Vision
**Question (AR)**: كيف تريد تحسين القسم؟ ما خطتك؟
**Question (EN)**: How would you improve your department? What is your vision?
**Question Type**: Long Text
**Ideas Must Be**:
- Specific (واضحة)
- Measurable (قابلة للقياس)
- Realistic (واقعية)
- Business-linked (مرتبطة بالأهداف)

**AI Assessment**: Leadership vision quality and strategic thinking

---

### Q18: 90-Day Implementation Plan
**Question (AR)**: ما الذي يمكن تنفيذه خلال الـ 90 يوم القادمة؟
**Question (EN)**: What can be realistically implemented in the next 90 days?
**Question Type**: Multi-item Plan
**For Each Initiative**:
- What (ماذا)
- Why (لماذا)
- How (كيف)
- Timeline
- Resources needed
- Expected impact

---

## DIMENSION 9: DECISION-MAKING AUTHORITY

### Q19: Decision-Making vs. Execution
**Question (AR)**: هل تتخذ القرارات أم تنفذ قرارات الآخرين؟
**Question (EN)**: Do you make decisions or execute decisions from others?
**Question Type**: Scale 1-10 + Text
**Scale**: 1 (Only execute decisions) to 10 (Only make decisions)
**Explanation**: Describe your decision-making authority percentage

---

### Q20: Top 3 Independent Decisions
**Question (AR)**: اذكر أكبر 3 قرارات تتخذها بشكل مستقل
**Question (EN)**: List your top 3 largest independent decisions
**Question Type**: Multi-item (3 decisions)
**For Each Decision**:
- Decision title
- Frequency (how often)
- Financial impact (if applicable)
- Risk involved
- Decision complexity (1-10 scale)
- Time to decide (hours/days/weeks)
- Approval needed (yes/no)

---

## QUESTION DIFFICULTY LEVELS

- **Easy**: Direct questions (Q1, Q9, Q10)
- **Medium**: Require explanation (Q2, Q6, Q15)
- **Complex**: Require detailed evidence (Q8, Q12, Q18, Q20)

Estimated time per dimension: 15-30 minutes
Total assessment time: 60-90 minutes
